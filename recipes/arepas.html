Arepas

Description:

Arepas are a type of cornmeal cake that is popular in Venezuela and Colombia. They can be grilled, baked, or fried and are often filled with various ingredients such as cheese, meats, or vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of pre-cooked cornmeal (masarepa)
  • 2 1/2 cups of warm water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil (optional)
  • Filling options: cheese, shredded beef, chicken, avocado, etc.

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the pre-cooked cornmeal and salt.
  2. Gradually add warm water to the cornmeal mixture, stirring until a dough forms. If using, add vegetable oil for extra flavor.
  3. Knead the dough for a few minutes until it is smooth and pliable.
  4. Divide the dough into equal portions and shape each portion into a round disc about 1/2 inch thick.
  5. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat. Cook each arepa for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Once cooked, slice open the arepas and fill them with your choice of ingredients.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy!
