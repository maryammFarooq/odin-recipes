Pikliz

Description:

Pikliz is a spicy Haitian pickled vegetable relish made with cabbage, carrots, and peppers. It is often served as a condiment with various dishes, adding a tangy and spicy flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup of grated carrots
  • 1/4 cup of sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup of sliced onions
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of hot sauce (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, grated carrots, sliced bell peppers, and sliced onions.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the salt, black pepper, vinegar, lime juice, hot sauce (if using), and garlic powder.
  3. Pour the dressing over the vegetable mixture and toss well to combine.
  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it sit in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Serve chilled as a side dish or condiment with your favorite Haitian dishes.
